Welcome to Stratus Technologies






Who are We?

We are Stratus technologies. We are an Irish software firm based in Cork Ireland.. Our company was founded by Jason Logue on 14th February 2018. We operate solely via our website to provide information and products for you, the customer.

What do we Do?

We are an Irish firm who specialise in knowledge of cloud technology. We offer up-to-date information on the latest cloud services and advancements in cloud technology. We also have a range of offers on various cloud services for very affordable prices.

Why Choose Us?

Although we may be a relatively new company, our team is well experienced in the field. All of our members have worked directly with cloud technology in the past and have expert understanding of the concept. They can offer expert advice and insight into the latest technologies available and their uses.
